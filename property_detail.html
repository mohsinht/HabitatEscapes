Overview
Howells Barn is an exceptionally well presented historic Cotswold stone barn set in private grounds within the acclaimed Lower Mill Estate.
The 1830s restored Cotswolds barn is set within approximately 2 acres of landscaped gardens with a private outdoor heated pool*, direct lake access and breath-taking views overlooking the lake and nature reserve. The Hide, accessed from the garden, is an elegant modernist steel-and-glass structure, voted Britain’s best small building in 2009, standing in complete seclusion, overlooking one of the largest lakes in the Cotswolds Water Park.
